The Purpose of Every Song

Each song carries a purpose.
Each purpose carries a story.

Lillie doesn't just release songs — she preserves moments. Behind every melody is a real conversation, a real season of life, a real prayer whispered in a quiet room. The Stories section is where those origins are recorded, shared, and kept for those who come after.

As each song-story is ready to be told, it will be added here — in Lillie's own words, with the honesty and care that shaped the music itself.

Featured Story

Teachers

This song was born in a moment of quiet gratitude — thinking back on every teacher who looked past the child I was and saw the person I could become. I remember sitting at my kitchen table, remembering a teacher's voice from forty years earlier, and realizing I had never thanked her.

"Teachers" became my way of saying thank you to every educator who has ever given a child more than a lesson — who gave them belief. It is one of the simplest songs I've written, and one of the ones closest to my heart.
