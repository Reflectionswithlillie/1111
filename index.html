In Her Own Words

I write because I believe
every experience has a lesson —
and every lesson has the power
to help someone else.

My songs are reflections of real life. The joy of new beginnings. The weight of grief. The quiet strength of faith. The complicated beauty of family. If one of my songs reaches you exactly when you need it — then every note was worth writing.
