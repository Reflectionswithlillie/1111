The Woman Behind the Music

About Lillie

Daughter. Wife. Mother. Grandmother. Songwriter. This is the story of how a lifetime of living became a lifetime of song.

My Story

A late-life calling, decades in the making.

I didn't set out to become a songwriter. For most of my life, music lived quietly inside me — in the hymns I sang as a girl, in the lullabies I hummed to my own children, in the prayers I whispered during seasons I never thought I'd survive.

It wasn't until later in life that the songs began to ask to be written down. Not for fame. Not for attention. But because I had lived enough, lost enough, healed enough, and learned enough to believe my reflections might matter to someone else.

"I write because I believe every experience in life has a lesson — and every lesson has the power to help someone else."

Reflections with Lillie is the result of that conviction. Every song you'll find here was born from something true — a marriage, a loss, a classroom, a kitchen table, a quiet morning with God. I don't write to entertain. I write to reach.

A Songwriter's Heart

Why I Write

I write because I believe that every experience in life has a lesson, and every lesson has the power to help someone else. My songs are reflections of real life — the good, the bad, the beautiful, and the painful.

If my words can encourage one heart, heal one soul, or make someone feel a little less alone, then every song is worth it. That is not a tagline. It is the only reason I sit down to write.

From Experience Every lyric traces back to something real — lived, not imagined.
With Purpose Written to encourage, uplift, and speak truth into real situations.
For You If you are walking through what I've walked through — you are not alone.
For Legacy Songs meant to outlive this moment, and reach the ones still to come.
